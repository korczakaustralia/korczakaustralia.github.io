News Flash

It is with great sadness we inform you of the passing of Itzak Belfer, the Last Korczak Boy, who for part of his childhood lived in Dom Sierot Orphanage in Warsaw and through his art and writing dedicated his life to Korczak.
Itzchak Belfer’s words of Korczak, "I lived in close proximity to him for ten years. I have lived my entire life in his light."
"I am now passing the story of this wonderful man on to you, before there is no one left to remember and share his story." Itzhak Belfer

JKAA Acknowledgement

The Janusz Korczak Association Australia acknowledges and pays respect to the past, present and future Traditional Custodians and Elders of this nation and the continuation of cultural, spiritual and educational practices of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples.

"Children aren't the people of tomorrow but are the people of today. They are entitled to be taken seriously. They have a right to be treated by adults with tenderess and respect, as equals. They should be allowed to grow into whoever they were meant to be- The unknown person inside each of them is the hope for the future."

Janusz Korczak
